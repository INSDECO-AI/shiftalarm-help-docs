ShiftAlarm user guide

Version 1. Date: 2026-06-24.

This page is a learning guide for ShiftAlarm. It explains what each main feature does, where to find it, what to tap and what to check if something does not work as expected.

1. First launch

On first launch, ShiftAlarm shows a short setup window. The main screen is already available behind it, but it is better to complete the basic setup first.

  1. Read the welcome message and tap “Continue”.
  2. On the documents step, open the documents or accept the required documents when you are ready.
  3. On the permissions step, open the permissions screen and check notifications, exact alarms, location and battery restrictions.
  4. Tap “Finish setup”.

You can return to permissions later from Settings → Permissions and reliability.

2. Permissions and reliability

Alarms, reminders and geo-alarms depend on Android and device vendor settings.

  • Notifications are needed for alarms, reminders, timer and service notifications.
  • Exact alarms help signals start at the planned time.
  • Location is needed for geo-alarms.
  • Background location is needed for geo-alarms when the app is closed.
  • Battery / background activity may affect reliability on Xiaomi/Redmi and some other devices.

Android does not always guarantee instant delivery of notifications, exact alarms and geofences. For important signals, check every item on the permissions screen.

3. Main screen

The main screen is the day panel. It provides quick access to clock, alarms, shift schedules, notes, geo-alarms and settings.

  • Tap the clock card to open clock, timer and stopwatch.
  • Tap alarms to open regular and shift-linked alarms.
  • Tap schedule to open the calendar and shift schedules.
  • Tap notes to open notes, events and reminders.
  • Tap geo-alarm to open the geo-alarm map.

4. Shift schedules

A shift schedule is used by the calendar, shift-linked alarms, salary calculation and calendar widgets.

  1. Open “Schedule”.
  2. Create a new schedule or edit an existing one.
  3. Set the schedule type, work days, shifts and colors.
  4. Make the required schedule active.

If a day differs from the regular pattern, use a calendar override: day off, sick leave, vacation, replacement or custom shift.

5. Alarms

ShiftAlarm supports regular alarms, weekday alarms, date alarms and shift-linked alarms.

  1. Open “Alarms”.
  2. Tap add alarm.
  3. Choose time, repeat mode, sound, vibration, duration, snooze and fade-in.
  4. For a shift-linked alarm, choose the shift mode and active schedule.
  5. Save the alarm.

For important alarms, check the permissions screen and avoid battery restrictions for the app.

6. Geo-alarms

A geo-alarm starts a signal when you enter a selected area, leave it, or do either action depending on the selected mode.

  1. Open “Geo alarm”.
  2. Long-press the required point on the map.
  3. Choose a radius. 800–1000 m is recommended for better reliability.
  4. Choose the trigger mode: enter, exit, or enter/exit.
  5. Configure sound, vibration, schedule and save.

On some devices, geofence delivery may be delayed because of battery saving, location accuracy or Android restrictions.

7. Notes, events and reminders

Notes help you store tasks, events, checklists, audio and reminders.

  • Note is a regular text note or structured blocks.
  • Event is a date-based item.
  • Reminder is an item with a notification at a selected time.
  • Audio is a voice recording inside notes.
  • Transcript converts audio to text when a model or system engine is available.

8. Clock, timer and stopwatch

The clock section contains world clocks, timer and stopwatch.

  • Add required cities and time zones in world clocks.
  • The timer can run in the background and show a notification.
  • The stopwatch can save sessions and laps.

If a timer or stopwatch is active, the main screen and widgets can show its state.

9. Widgets

ShiftAlarm supports Android home screen widgets.

  • Today 4×2 is a compact panel with two configurable blocks.
  • Today 4×3 has a clock block on top and two blocks below.
  • Calendar 4×4 widgets show shift calendar information.

Add a widget from the Android launcher widget menu.

10. Salary

The salary section helps estimate income from shifts, bonuses, sales and deductions.

  • Configure base rates and calculation settings.
  • Add constant deductions if they repeat every month.
  • Add variable deductions if they only apply to the current month.
  • For sales, use a common percentage or categories with different percentages.

Salary calculation is an auxiliary tool. Always compare the result with employer rules and official payroll documents before using it as final data.

11. Backup and restore

Backup stores user settings and app data.

  1. Open Settings → Backup.
  2. Tap “Save” / “Download” to save the file to the phone.
  3. Tap “Share” / “Send” to send the file through the system share sheet.
  4. To restore, select a backup file.

Keep a backup in a safe place, especially before reinstalling the app or changing phones.

12. Diagnostics and errors

The app includes diagnostic logs for separate features. They help understand why something did not work.

  1. Open Settings → Diagnostics.
  2. Choose the required section: alarms, clock, shifts, notes, geo-alarm and others.
  3. Copy the log and send it to the developer together with a description of the problem.

The more precise the description and time of the problem are, the easier it is to find the cause.

13. FAQ

An alarm did not ring on time

Check notifications, exact alarms, background activity and battery restrictions.

A geo-alarm was delayed

Increase radius to 800–1000 m, enable precise and background location, and check battery settings.

A widget did not update immediately

Open the app or tap the refresh button on the widget. Android may limit frequent widget updates.

I changed the language, but the document or guide is not updated yet

Check internet connection and wait for download. If the remote version is unavailable, the app uses the bundled fallback.

This guide may be updated through GitHub. If the remote version is unavailable, the app uses the bundled offline version.
